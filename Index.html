What is "Area 51"?

“Area 51” or “Зона 51” is the first live action-quest game, upgraded into a whole new level of interaction with the latest special effects and action, allowing you to feel what no video game could offer you!

How long does the game last?

60min.

How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website, where you will see which hours of which days are free on our calendar, the white-coloured hours are free, and those in red – taken.

How many people should we be?

The recommended number for a group is from two to five people, the difficulty of the game is consisted with those criteria.

Is there an age limit?

Because of the thematic and nature of the game, we do not recommend it for people, under the age of 16, whereas in every group there must be at least one player or guardian of age.

Is our game being recorded?

Your game is not being recorded. You are, however, being monitored by an operator at all time.

In the event of an emergency, can I exit the room?

Yes, you can exit the room at any time, you will be escorted out and the rest of your group could continue playing.

Is there anything scary?

Yes!... and no, of course. Everything in the room is props and fake, danger for you and your teammates does not exist nowhere and at no time whatsoever. We try to get you into a “real story” and to make you the main characters in it, in our effort we have tried to keep a certain ambiance and mood to keep the story unveiling in an interesting way.
